# Meta-Union Firewall

**Status:** companion boundary guide. Canon controls if conflict occurs.

## Purpose

MathGov includes philosophical horizon language such as the All-Encompassing Infinite Union (AIU) and meta-union concepts. These can orient humility and long-range responsibility, but they must not be confused with Tier 1-3 computation.

## Firewall rule

All-Encompassing Infinite Union (AIU), Cosmic, Universal, and other horizon or meta-union language may orient philosophical interpretation, humility, and long-range ethical imagination. They do not enter Tier 1-3 gate passage, RLS ranking, legal authority, deployment permission, ProofPack claims, or reference-calculator outputs unless a future governed extension explicitly defines a computable protocol and its validation status.

## Allowed Tier 1-3 uses

- humility marker;
- philosophical horizon;
- public meaning layer;
- non-disposability reminder;
- prompt to avoid premature scope closure.

## Prohibited Tier 1-3 uses

- numeric RLS cell;
- hidden weight;
- gate override;
- authority claim;
- empirical proof claim;
- legal certification;
- deployment permission;
- selection tie-break unless formally specified in a governed future extension.

## Plain-language rule

All-Encompassing Infinite Union (AIU) may guide the horizon. It does not secretly score the run.
