## Core cascade

The public RippleLogic method is:

**Reality Grounding → Rights Floor → TRC → CSV → RLS**

Formal shorthand:

**RG/RSG → RF/NCRC → TRC → CSV → RLS**

Where:

- **Reality Grounding** establishes what is known, unknown, evidenced, and claimable.
- **Rights Floor / NCRC** protects non-compensatory rights before welfare scoring.
- **TRC** bounds catastrophic and irreversible tail risks.
- **CSV** checks Containment and Structural Viability.
- **RLS** ranks only options that survive the upstream levels.
