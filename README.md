## Core Cascade

**Reality Grounding → Rights Floor → TRC → CSV → RLS**

Where:

- **Reality Grounding** verifies contact with the real situation, evidence, constraints, uncertainty, and category choice.
- **Rights Floor** prevents optimization from overriding protected rights.
- **TRC**, the **Tail-Risk Constraint**, bounds catastrophic and irreversible downside risk before benefit scoring.
- **CSV**, **Core Structure Viability**, checks whether the action preserves the basic structures needed for life, agency, trust, governance, and long-term flourishing.
- **RLS**, **RippleLogic Scoring**, evaluates multi-level ripple effects after the prior constraints are satisfied.

Plain-language version:

1. Ground reality.
2. Protect rights.
3. Bound ruin.
4. Preserve the structure.
5. Score the ripples.

A high benefit score cannot compensate for a failed Rights Floor, failed tail-risk constraint, or failed core-structure viability check.

## What RippleLogic Is

RippleLogic is the MathGov decision engine.

It evaluates candidate actions through a strict five-stage method:

1. **Reality Grounding**
2. **Rights Floor**
3. **Tail-Risk Constraint**
4. **Core Structure Viability**
5. **RippleLogic Scoring**

In formal shorthand:

```text
RG → RF → TRC → CSV → RLS

The central discipline is sequencing.

Reality, rights, catastrophic risk, and structural viability are checked before final benefit scoring. This prevents a system from using attractive aggregate outcomes to hide rights violations, catastrophic risk, or damage to the conditions that make flourishing possible.

Reality Grounding

MathGov defines reality, for decision-making, as the evaluator-independent field of constrained structures that, under transition, produce consequences.

A decision never captures the whole territory. It captures a reality surface. Evidence is traceable contact with that surface. Claims must remain inside the boundary of that contact. Where the surface is insufficient for a material claim, the system must narrow, escalate, handle conservatively, or refuse.

Public teaching line:

Reality is the territory. Structure is its arrangement. Constraint is its law. Consequence is its answer. Evidence is our contact. Ethics is how we act without pretending the surface is the whole.

Category Grounding and Primitive Audit are required subdisciplines of Reality Grounding when category choice materially affects rights, tail risk, core-structure viability, SGP interpretation, authority, conformance, or public claims.
